About

The Healthcare Recommendation System leverages reinforcement learning to provide personalized health recommendations based on patient symptoms and historical data. By continuously learning from user interactions and feedback, the system adapts to individual health profiles, ensuring tailored advice that evolves over time. This innovative approach not only enhances patient engagement by offering relevant self-care suggestions and emergency contacts but also improves the efficiency of healthcare providers by streamlining decision-making processes.
